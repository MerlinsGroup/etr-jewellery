Custom Jewellery

Book your appointment for custom-made jewellery. VVS diamonds, solid gold, and bespoke designs made just for you.

The Process

Every custom jewellery order starts with a consultation. We'll discuss your design, understand your vision, and craft a piece that's uniquely yours.

1

Book Your Consultation

Select a date and time that works for you. Consultations take approximately 30 minutes.

2

Design Discussion

We'll discuss your vision, materials, and provide a quote based on your requirements.

3

Design & Approval

We'll create a design mockup for your approval before beginning the crafting process.

4

Crafting & Delivery

Your custom jewellery will be handcrafted and delivered within 2-3 weeks.

Pricing

Custom jewellery pricing varies based on design complexity and materials. VVS diamond pieces and bespoke designs are quoted during consultation based on your specifications.
