Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom jewellery design?

Yes, we specialise in bespoke jewellery design. Our master craftsmen work closely with you to bring your vision to life, from initial concept sketches to the final masterpiece.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original condition. Custom-made pieces are final sale, but we ensure your complete satisfaction throughout the design process.

Do you provide jewellery repair services?

Absolutely. Our skilled artisans can repair, restore, and resize your precious pieces. We handle everything from simple cleanings to complex restorations of antique jewellery.

How long does shipping take?

Standard UK delivery takes 2-3 business days. International shipping typically takes 5-10 business days depending on the destination. All orders are fully insured and require a signature upon delivery.
